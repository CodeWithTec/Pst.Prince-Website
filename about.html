Our Journey

Over three decades of excellence in teacher education

1985

Founded as a small teacher training institute with just 50 students and 5 faculty members.

1995

Received national accreditation and expanded to offer Bachelor's degree programs.

2005

Opened new campus and introduced Master's degree programs in Education.

2015

Launched online learning programs and modern teaching facilities.

2023

Celebrated 5000+ alumni and ranked top teacher training college nationally.

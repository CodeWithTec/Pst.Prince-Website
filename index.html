Teaching & Learning Approach

Our training combines theory and practice through:

Interactive lectures

Instructional sessions that break up traditional lecturing with active learning, encouraging student participation and engagement with material.

Artive
Micro-teaching sessions

Structured, scaled-down teacher training workshops designed to improve specific pedagogical skills.

Artive
Group discussions

A structured, interactive conversation among a small number of people aimed at analyzing a specific topic, sharing perspectives, or solving a problem.

Artive
Teaching demonstrations

A practical, visual method where an educator shows students how to perform a skill, process, or concept step-by-step.

Artive
Supervised Teaching Practice

A compulsory, mentored component of teacher training where a student teacher (trainee) conducts lessons in a real classroom, guided by a qualified mentor teacher and university staff.

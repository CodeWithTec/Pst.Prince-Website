Dr. Sarah Johnson

Professor of Education

Biography

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a distinguished professor with over 25 years of experience in teacher education. She received her Ph.D. in Educational Leadership from Stanford University and has been instrumental in developing innovative curriculum frameworks used in teacher preparation programs nationwide.

Research Interests
  • Teacher Preparation and Development
  • Curriculum Design and Evaluation
  • Educational Leadership
  • Equity in Education

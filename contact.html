Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I schedule a campus tour?
You can schedule a campus tour by calling our admissions office at (555) 123-4568 or by filling out the contact form above. Tours are available Monday through Friday at 10 AM and 2 PM, and Saturdays at 11 AM.
What are the application deadlines?
Fall Semester: August 15
Spring Semester: December 15
Summer Session: May 1
Early decision and priority deadlines are also available. Check our admissions page for details.
Do you offer online programs?
Yes, we offer several online programs including our Online Teaching Certificate and select graduate courses. Some programs are offered in a hybrid format combining online and on-campus learning.
How can I get financial aid information?
Our financial aid office is available Monday-Friday 9 AM-5 PM. You can email financialaid@ttc.edu or call (555) 123-4570 to speak with a counselor. We also offer virtual appointments.
What documents do I need to apply?
Required documents include: completed application form, official transcripts, personal statement, letters of recommendation, and application fee. Graduate programs may require additional documents.
Is parking available on campus?
Yes, visitors can park in the visitor parking lot located at the north entrance of campus. Parking permits can be obtained at the security booth. Free parking is available on weekends.
